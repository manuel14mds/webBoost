About Us

WE ARE THE EMERALD SPECIALISTS. We have a large selection of exclusive Emerald jewelry including Pendants, Earrings, Bracelets and exquisite rings for both men and woman.

We also carry a special line of unpolished (rough) emerald jewelry.

We have a collection of loose emeralds from 1 to 50 Carats available.

Our unique designs are in 18cr gold and we have our own jewelers who can custom make anything you desire. All your jewelry is accompanied with a Certificate of Guarantee.
